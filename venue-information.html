Information on how to get to University of Technology, Sydney (UTS)

University of Technology, Sydney city campus is right next to Central station, the main train and light rail hub for the Sydney CBD, as well as the Railway Square bus interchange.

See on the train trip planning website how you can travel from the airport to UTS.

Information on how to get to Australian Computer Society (ACS)

Australian Computer Society is next to Sydney Metro. Barangaroo Metro Station is located beneath the northern end of Hickson Road, south of Munn Street in Millers Point.

See on the train trip planning website how you can travel from the airport to ACS.
