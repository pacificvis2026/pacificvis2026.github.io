Information on how to get to University of Technology, Sydney (UTS)

University of Technology, Sydney city campus is a short walk from Central Station, the main train and Metro hub for the Sydney CBD.

See how to walk from Central Station to UTS

Information on how to get to Australian Computer Society (ACS)

Australian Computer Society is a short walk from the to Barangaroo Metro Station. Barangaroo Metro Station is located beneath the northern end of Hickson Road, south of Munn Street in Millers Point. You can take the Metro from Central station. The Metro is not the same as a train. If you are coming from the International Airport you will need to get off the train at Central and change to the Metro.

See how to walk from Barangaroo Metro Station to ACS
